Section 17

BPSC Trap Patterns — World Geography Final Closer

15 most common traps the closer adds — read before exam
⚠ TRAP 1 — Saar Coal vs Iron Ore
"Match: Kimberley-Diamond, Witwatersrand-Gold, Katanga-Copper, Saar-Iron Ore. Which is incorrect?"
Answer: Saar-Iron Ore is INCORRECT. Saar (Germany) is famous for COAL, not iron ore. This exact trap appeared in past BPSC.
⚠ TRAP 2 — Uranium Producer Confusion
"Uranium - South Africa: Correct or Incorrect?"
Answer: INCORRECT. Top uranium producers are Kazakhstan (42%), Canada, Australia. South Africa is NOT a top uranium producer. (BPSC 72nd asked this exact trap.)
⚠ TRAP 3 — Commercial Dairy vs Total Milk
"Which country leads in commercial dairy farming?"
Answer: New Zealand (commercial dairy specialization). India leads in TOTAL milk production due to large cattle population, but NOT in commercial dairy farming. Read the question type carefully.
⚠ TRAP 4 — Oil Production vs Oil Reserves
"Country with largest oil reserves vs largest oil producer?"
Reserves: Venezuela. Producer: USA (shale oil). Don't confuse the two.
⚠ TRAP 5 — Cape of Good Hope vs Cape Agulhas
"What is the southernmost point of Africa?"
Answer: Cape Agulhas, NOT Cape of Good Hope. Cape of Good Hope is more famous historically (shipping route) but Agulhas is geographically southernmost.
⚠ TRAP 6 — White Nile vs Blue Nile Source
"Source of White Nile is Lake Tana — true or false?"
Answer: FALSE. White Nile source = Lake Victoria. Blue Nile source = Lake Tana. Common BPSC swap-trap.
⚠ TRAP 7 — Indonesia New Capital
"What is Indonesia's new capital and where?"
Answer: Nusantara, located in East Kalimantan (Borneo island). NOT Java (where Jakarta is). The shift addresses sinking + congestion of Jakarta.
⚠ TRAP 8 — Largest Island Continent Trap
"Is Australia the largest island in the world?"
Answer: NO — Australia is a CONTINENT, not an island. Largest island is Greenland. Australia is also a country and continent.
⚠ TRAP 9 — Highest Volcano vs Largest Volcano
"World's highest volcano vs largest active volcano?"
Highest: Ojos del Salado (Chile-Argentina, 6,893m). Largest active: Mauna Loa (Hawaii). These are NOT the same.
⚠ TRAP 10 — Tallest vs Widest vs Largest Waterfall
"World's largest waterfall is Angel Falls — true or false?"
Answer: FALSE. Angel Falls is TALLEST (979m). LARGEST BY VOLUME is Inga Falls (Congo). WIDEST is Khone Falls (Laos). Read the question type.
⚠ TRAP 11 — Iceland on Ring of Fire?
"Iceland's volcanoes are part of the Pacific Ring of Fire — true or false?"
Answer: FALSE. Iceland is on the Mid-Atlantic Ridge (divergent plate boundary), NOT the Pacific Ring of Fire. Both are volcanic zones but different.
⚠ TRAP 12 — Bauxite Top Producer
"Largest bauxite producer is?"
Answer: Australia (~25% of world). Not China (which has reserves but Australia leads production). Note: this was BPSC 72nd's exact pair.
⚠ TRAP 13 — Trans-Afghan Railway Endpoints
"Trans-Afghan Railway connects Uzbekistan to which country?"
Answer: Pakistan (via Afghanistan to reach Arabian Sea ports). NOT Iran or India. This was BPSC 72nd's exact question.
⚠ TRAP 14 — Kyaukpyu Port Developer
"Kyaukpyu Port in Myanmar is being developed by?"
Answer: China. Part of China-Myanmar Economic Corridor (CMEC) — gives China Indian Ocean access bypassing Malacca Strait.
⚠ TRAP 15 — Diamond Producer (Volume vs Value)
"World's largest diamond producer?"
By volume: Russia (~31%). By value: Botswana (~20%, higher quality). Both can be correct depending on question framing.
